# Converter Image to PDF - Privacy Policy (public version)

**Effective date:** March 12, 2026  
**Developer / Publisher:** Ameda Studios  
**App:** Converter Image to PDF

## 1. Overview
Converter Image to PDF is a mobile application that allows users to convert images to PDF, convert PDF pages to images, merge PDF files, preview documents, and export generated files from their device.

## 2. Data processing model
Core file conversion features are processed locally on the device. The developer does not upload user images or PDF files to a developer-controlled server for these core conversion actions.

## 3. Permissions
### Camera
The app requests camera access only when the user chooses to take a photo inside the app for conversion to PDF.

### Photos / files
The app uses system pickers and user-selected export locations whenever possible. The app is configured to avoid broad photo access on Android. Users choose the specific images or documents they want to process.

## 4. Information that may be processed
Depending on the feature used, the app may process:
- Images selected by the user
- PDF files selected by the user
- Generated PDF or PNG files
- Local history metadata needed to show previously generated files
- Premium subscription status
- Advertising and diagnostic data processed by third-party SDKs

## 5. Advertising
The app uses Google AdMob. Google and its partners may process data such as IP address, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, diagnostic information, and ad interaction data to provide, measure, and protect advertising services. This third-party processing is governed by Google's privacy terms and documentation.

## 6. Purchases and subscriptions
The app offers premium features through Google Play Billing. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. The developer does not receive or store the user's full payment card information through the app.

## 7. Storage, retention, and deletion
Generated files may remain on the user's device until they are deleted by the user. Temporary files may be stored in app storage during processing. Local history entries may be deleted by the user from within the app. Third-party services such as Google AdMob and Google Play Billing may retain certain data according to their own policies.

## 8. Sharing of information
The developer does not sell user files. Data may be shared with third-party providers only to the extent required for app functionality, subscription handling, legal compliance, security, diagnostics, or ad delivery.

## 9. Security
Reasonable technical measures are used to reduce unnecessary access to user data, including limiting requested permissions and performing core conversions locally where applicable. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 10. Children's privacy
The app is not specifically directed to children unless the store listing and in-app experience are configured and disclosed accordingly.

## 11. Changes to this policy
This policy may be updated from time to time. Material updates should be reflected in the public privacy policy URL and, where appropriate, in the app listing.
